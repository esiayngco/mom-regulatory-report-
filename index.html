The Scope at a Glance

From 2023 through 2026, Evergreen Health Monroe accumulated 57 documented findings across DNV accreditation and DOH state surveys. DNV surveys occur on a rolling cycle and focus on clinical governance, life safety, and operational systems. DOH surveys tend to be complaint-driven or focused and produced a cluster of findings in 2024.

Findings Over Time

DNV vs. DOH — Different Lenses

DNV and DOH approach hospital oversight very differently. DNV's accreditation model emphasizes systems-based compliance — fire safety, medical staff credentials, and operational documentation. DOH surveys are more likely to flag patient safety events and procedural failures at the point of care. Click either bar to filter all charts below.

Insight: DNV accounts for 63% of all findings (36 of 57), largely due to its continuous annual survey cycle. DOH's 21 findings in this window were concentrated in a single 2024 survey visit with a broad scope.
